C. Power Calculations

Gateway Power Requirements

Solar Panel Sizing = 144Wh × 1.3 (safety factor) = 187.2Wh

Required Panel Rating = 187.2Wh ÷ 5 hours peak sun = 37.44W

Selected Panel Size = 100W for redundancy

Battery Capacity Calculations
