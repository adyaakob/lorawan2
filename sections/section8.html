A. Technical Specifications

End Node Specifications (HKT GT-100)

  • Model: HKT GT-100 Waterproof GPS LoRa Tracker
  • Dimensions: 100×33×20mm
  • Weight: 80g (including battery)
  • GPS Module: High sensitivity (-148dB)
  • LoRa Protocol: Standard LoRaWAN Class A
  • Battery Type: Rechargeable Li-ion, 1000mAh
  • Operating Current:
    • Sleep mode: <10μA
    • Active mode: <20mA
  • Battery Life: 2-3 years with typical usage
  • Operating Temperature: -20°C to +65°C

Gateway Specifications (Milesight SG50)

  • Battery: Built-in rechargeable 25Ah battery
  • Solar Panel: 30W (upgradeable to 45W)
  • Operating Temperature: -30°C to +70°C
  • Enclosure Rating: IP67 rated
  • Capacity: Supports up to 2000 end-nodes
  • Coverage Range: Up to 15km in rural areas
  • Power Consumption: 0.8W typical
  • LoRa Receiver: 8-channel

Optional VSAT Specifications (Hughes 9502)

  • Type: IP-based VSAT solution
  • Data Rates: Up to 448 kbps standard IP
  • Power Consumption: <20W transmit, <1.5W idle
  • Operating Temperature: -40°C to +75°C
  • Additional Power Requirements:
    • 100W solar panel
    • 100Ah battery backup

C. Power Calculations

Gateway Power Requirements

Daily Power Consumption (SG50):
0.8W × 24 hours = 19.2Wh/day

Solar Panel Output:
30W × 5 hours peak sun = 150Wh/day

Battery Backup:
25Ah × 12V = 300Wh (4 days backup)

VSAT Power Requirements (Optional)

VSAT Daily Consumption:
(20W × 4h) + (1.5W × 20h) = 110Wh/day

Combined Daily Load:
19.2Wh + 110Wh = 129.2Wh/day

Required Solar Panel: 100W minimum

Additional Battery: 100Ah for VSAT operation
